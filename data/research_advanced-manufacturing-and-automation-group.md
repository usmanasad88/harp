# Advanced Manufacturing and Automation Group — Mechatronics Engineering, NUST CEME

The Advanced Manufacturing and Automation Group of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering, NUST CEME, works on manufacturing technologies and automation. Members: Dr Amir Hamza (Associate Professor — engineering materials), Dr Anas bin Aqeel (Assistant Professor — additive manufacturing), and Usman Asad (Senior Lecturer — control systems).

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/research/advanced-manufacturing-and-automation-group (fetched 2026-07-19)

## Advanced Manufacturing and Automation Group — facilities and funding

Capabilities and facilities: 5-axis CNC machining centre, universal drill and milling machine, FDM and SLA 3D printing, 3D scanning, PCB prototyping, laser cutting for metals and non-metals, composite manufacturing, and licensed CAD modelling software.

Funding: establishment of the Robot Maker Lab under NCRA (PKR 118 million), and "Design and development of Agribot for local agriculture applications" (PKR 3.2 million).
