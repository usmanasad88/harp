# Mechatronics programme educational objectives (PEOs) — NUST CEME

Graduates of the Mechatronics Engineering programme at NUST College of EME will:

- PEO 1 — Be employable mechatronic engineers who are knowledgeable, skillful and able to solve complex engineering problems.
- PEO 2 — Have an inclination towards research and lifelong learning, and be able to promote entrepreneurial ideas.
- PEO 3 — Be effective engineers with leadership qualities and high morals and professional ethics.
