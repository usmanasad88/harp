# Mechatronics Engineering course curriculum — where to find it

The full course curriculum for the Bachelor of Mechatronics Engineering at NUST College of EME is published on the CEME website under Programs (https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/programs), with separate curriculum documents per intake year (Fall 2020 onward, Fall 2023, Fall 2024, Fall 2025 and onward). The programme combines mechanical engineering, electronics, embedded systems, control, computing and AI coursework.
