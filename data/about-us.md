# About NUST College of EME (CEME)

The College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (CEME) is a constituent college of the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), located on Peshawar Road, Rawalpindi. It is a prime hub of engineering education in Pakistan, offering leading undergraduate and graduate programmes.

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/about-us (fetched 2026-07-19)

## History of CEME — from EME School to college

The Corps of Electrical and Mechanical Engineers (EME) maintains the mechanical and electrical equipment of the Pakistan Army. To meet the Corps' training requirements, the Electrical and Mechanical Engineers School was established at Quetta on 1 April 1957, and by 1969 it was elevated to the College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering. The college started its own BSc Engineering programme in 1981 and moved to its present Rawalpindi campus in April 1984, the same year revitalizing its BSc programme in affiliation with UET Lahore. Graduates have won Rhodes, Commonwealth and S&T scholarships. MSc programmes began in 1988 for the Corps of Signals and EME, extending to the Air Force, Navy and civil organizations in 1989; from 1990 entry to the BSc programme changed to technical cadets. NUST itself was established in March 1991 for the promotion of higher scientific education in Pakistan.
