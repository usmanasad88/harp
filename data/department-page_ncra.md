# NCRA at the Department of Mechatronics Engineering — links

The National Centre of Robotics and Automation (NCRA) is headquartered at the NUST College of E&ME and closely tied to the Department of Mechatronics Engineering. NCRA website: https://ncra.org.pk — College NCRA page: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/ncra. NCRA is a consortium of 11 labs at 13 universities across Pakistan (full details in the main NCRA document).
