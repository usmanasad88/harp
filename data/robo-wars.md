# RoboWars Championship — Indus RAS Expo 2026

RoboWars is the combat-robotics championship of Indus RAS Expo 2026, held 22–23 July 2026 at the Pakistan China Friendship Centre, Islamabad. Robots built with reinforced armour and powerful weapons fight no-holds-barred battles in an arena — crushing impacts, flying sparks, roaring motors and spectacular knockouts — until only the toughest machine earns the title of champion. Matches run on Day 1 from 2:00 to 5:00 pm and on Day 2 from 10:00 am to 6:00 pm, with intervals.

> Source: https://indusrasexpo.gov.pk/robo-wars (fetched 2026-07-19)

## RoboWars prize money

The total prize money for the RoboWars Championship is PKR 2,000,000 (2 million Pakistani rupees).
