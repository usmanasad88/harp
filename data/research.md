# CEME Research Groups — NUST College of EME

Research at the NUST College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (CEME) is organized into research groups across departments, coordinated with the Office of Research, Innovation and Commercialization (ORIC).

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/research (fetched 2026-07-19)

## Mechatronics Engineering research groups at CEME

The Department of Mechatronics Engineering hosts six research groups: the Bio Robotics Group, Unmanned Systems Group, Agrobotics Research Group, Micro and Nano Robotics Group, Advanced Manufacturing and Automation Group, and the Industrial AI and IoT (IAI) Research Group. Each group has its own members, featured projects and funding (covered in separate documents).

## Other CEME research groups

Other groups across the college include: Vision and Machine Learning (VML); Biomedical Signals and Systems (BSS); BioMedical Image and Signal Analysis (BIOMISA); Microwave and Antennas (MICA); Digital Signal Processing and Radar Systems (DiSPR); Semiconductor Electronics and Optics (SEO); Sustainable Energy, Smart Grid and Power Systems (SESGPS); Cognitive Radio and Wireless Communication (CORWIC); Control Systems and Robotics (CSR); Sustainable Energy Analytics Lab (SEAL); Computational Mechanics; Advanced Materials and Manufacturing Systems; Emerging Technology Lab (ETL); Advanced Automotive, Dynamics and Controls; Fluid Mechanics; Supply Chain Analytics and Circular Economy; Technology Management and Engineering Education; Project and Operations Management; plus mathematics and physics groups (Differential Equations, Computational Mathematics, Graph Theory and Cryptography, Astrophysics and Cosmology).
