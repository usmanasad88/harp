# Mechatronics Engineering faculty achievements — NUST CEME

Notable recognitions and achievements of the faculty of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering, NUST College of EME.

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/department-page/achievements-6 (fetched 2026-07-19)

## Dr Umar Shahbaz Khan — Pride of Performance and curriculum development

Prof. Dr Umar Shahbaz Khan received the Pride of Performance award (2024). He is a Professor at NUST CEME and Director of the National Centre of Robotics and Automation (NCRA). His achievements include accreditation of Mechatronics Engineering as one of the first programmes under the Washington Accord, which helped the Pakistan Engineering Council become a signatory; project fundraising of around PKR 585 million; development of the Myo Prosthetic Upper Limb, now commercialized and widely used by patients; co-founding two NUST-approved spinoffs; and 78 publications. He was also honored with a Certificate of Appreciation for contributions to the undergraduate Mechatronics curriculum as a co-opted expert of the Pakistan Engineering Council's ECRDC, and won NUST Top Performer Awards for most submitted project proposals and most industrial projects acquired.

## Dr Hassan Elahi — world's top 2% scientists

Dr Hassan Elahi, Associate Professor of Mechatronics Engineering, has been recognized among the world's top 2% scientists by Stanford University and Elsevier, highlighting his exceptional contributions to scientific research and innovation.

## Dr Ayesha Zeb — Best Teacher Award and AI fruit quality assessment

Dr Ayesha Zeb received the NUST Best Teacher Award, a competitive selection across department, institution and university levels among more than 1,300 faculty members. With Dr Mohsin Islam Tiwana she developed an acclaimed AI-driven automated decision-support system for non-destructive fruit quality assessment, published in Springer Nature and Elsevier journals, recognized at CxO Global, Express Tribune and the Felix Instruments newsletter, and covered by ARY News, PTV News and other outlets.

## Dr Tahir Habib Nawaz — community service and international lab

Dr Tahir Habib Nawaz was awarded the Best Community Service Award, and received a NUST Top Performer Award for establishing a joint international lab at SINES.

## Mechatronics faculty book — Handbook of Manufacturing Systems and Design

Dr Uzair Khaleeq uz Zaman, Assistant Professor Kanwal Naveed, and Assistant Professor Anas Bin Aqeel, with former CEME Dean Dr Aamer Baqai, co-authored the "Handbook of Manufacturing Systems and Design: An Industry 4.0 Perspective", published by Taylor & Francis Group, bridging academia and industry.

## Dr Danish Hussain — book chapters in SPR Nanoscience

Dr Danish Hussain, with PhD student Masood ur Rehman and Nazo Haroon, published two book chapters in SPR Nanoscience, including "Soft-magnetic microrobotics: nanomaterials, fabrications and biomedical applications" (Royal Society of Chemistry, published 4 September 2024, DOI 10.1039/BK9781837674138-00140).
