# Exhibit at Indus RAS Expo 2026 — exhibitor registration

Companies, startups and institutions can showcase their products and services at Indus RAS Expo 2026. Exhibitor registration options are available on the expo website at https://indusrasexpo.gov.pk/exhibit-now. The expo runs 22–23 July 2026 at the Pakistan China Friendship Centre, Islamabad.
