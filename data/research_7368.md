# Industrial AI and IoT (IAI) Research Group — Mechatronics Engineering, NUST CEME

The Industrial AI and IoT (IAI) Research Group of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering, NUST CEME, tackles real-world industrial challenges with state-of-the-art AI. It equips members with expertise in AI and the Internet of Things (IoT) through hands-on training, focusing on agentic AI, generative AI, deep learning, reinforcement learning, IoT and human-machine interaction, producing research with tangible benefits for local industry in Pakistan. ## IAI Research Group — featured projects

- HARP — Humanoid Assistive Robotic Platform (the group's humanoid receptionist/assistant robot)
- SceneScribe — AI-enabled intelligent glasses for assistance of visually impaired people
- Intelligent Prosthetic Hand — a 3D-printed solution combining soft robotics, sensor fusion and computer vision
- CleanBot — autonomous indoor cleaning robot
- Smart fruit harvesting robot based on the UR5 manipulator
- Autovendi — autonomous food delivery robot
- Electrospin — a sustainable smart transport solution
