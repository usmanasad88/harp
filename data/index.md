# Indus RAS Expo 2026 — Quick Facts

Indus RAS Expo 2026 is Pakistan's national platform for Robotics and Autonomous Systems (RAS), a government-led initiative held on Wednesday 22nd and Thursday 23rd July 2026 at the Pakistan China Friendship Centre, Garden Avenue, Shakarparian Road, Islamabad. Day 1 is open to all visitors from 1:00 pm to 6:00 pm (the morning is an invitation-only opening ceremony); Day 2 is open to all visitors from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm.

> Source: https://indusrasexpo.gov.pk (fetched 2026-07-19)

## Indus RAS Expo — dates, venue and location

The expo takes place on 22 and 23 July 2026 at the Pakistan China Friendship Centre, Garden Avenue, Shakarparian Road, Islamabad, Pakistan. Highlights include a RoboWars championship, live drone shows, hands-on robotics workshops, panel discussions, plenary sessions, and an exhibition of robotics and autonomous-systems companies, universities and startups.
