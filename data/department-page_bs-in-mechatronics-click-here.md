# Mechatronics Engineering admissions — BS, MS and PhD

Admissions to the BS in Mechatronics Engineering, MS in Mechatronics Engineering, and PhD in Mechatronics Engineering at NUST College of EME are handled through the NUST admissions system. Application links for each programme are on the department's admissions page at https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/department-page/bs-in-mechatronics-click-here, and general admission information is at https://nust.edu.pk. Undergraduate admission is via the NUST entry test (NET).
