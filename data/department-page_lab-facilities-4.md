# Mechatronics Engineering lab facilities — NUST College of EME

The Department of Mechatronics Engineering at NUST College of EME has thirteen labs:

1. Circuit Design Lab
2. Electronics Lab
3. Machine Vision and Embedded Systems Lab
4. CAD/CAM Lab
5. Computer Lab
6. Industrial Automation Lab
7. Robotics and Control Lab
8. Robot Maker Lab
9. Robot Design and Development Lab
10. Micro & Nano Robotics Technologies Lab
11. Final Year Project Lab
12. FAIZ PLC Lab
13. Instrumentation and Sensing Lab

The Robot Maker Lab and Robot Design and Development Lab are also NCRA (National Centre of Robotics and Automation) facilities.
