# Mechatronics Engineering department events — NERC, ICRAI and ASEAN Expo

The Department of Mechatronics Engineering at NUST College of EME organizes major national and international robotics events, including the National Engineering Robotics Contest (NERC) and the International Conference on Robotics and Automation in Industry (ICRAI).

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/department-page/events-6 (fetched 2026-07-19)

## NERC — National Engineering Robotics Contest

The National Engineering Robotics Contest (NERC) is organized annually by the Department of Mechatronics Engineering at EME College, NUST, attracting universities and colleges from across Pakistan. Participants showcase innovation and technical prowess in categories including Indigenous, Modular, RoboWars, and Aero. NERC has become a prestigious platform fostering robotics and engineering skills among students, encouraging collaboration and creativity among the nation's budding engineers.

## ICRAI — International Conference on Robotics and Automation in Industry

ICRAI is organized by the Department of Mechatronics Engineering, NUST College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (CEME), Rawalpindi — one of Pakistan's leading international forums for robotics and automation researchers. The conference was held in 2012, 2016, 2019 and 2021 at NUST CEME, in 2023 at UET Peshawar, and in 2024 again at NUST CEME.

## ASEAN–Pakistan Technology Expo 2025 at NUST

The ASEAN–Pakistan Technology Expo 2025 was held at NUST Islamabad, jointly organized by the National Centre of Robotics and Automation (NCRA at NUST) and the ASEAN Committee on Science, Technology and Innovation (COSTI). It welcomed 18 international delegates from ASEAN member states and over 600 participants from across Pakistan. The programme featured keynotes, panel discussions, an exhibition where all National Centres of Excellence and over 35 tech companies and startups showcased solutions, technical breakout sessions, and visits to NUST schools and the National Science and Technology Park (NSTP). Chief guest Federal Minister for Planning Prof Ahsan Iqbal Chaudhry called for enhanced ASEAN–Pakistan academic exchanges and investment in robotics, AI, cybersecurity, big data, sustainable energy and agri-tech. Dr Umar Shahbaz Khan, Director NCRA, highlighted the initiative under the centre's international outreach programme.
