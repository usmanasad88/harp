# Mechatronics Engineering funded projects — NUST CEME

The Department of Mechatronics Engineering at NUST College of EME has attracted over PKR 1,334 million in research funding. Major funders include HEC (through NCRA, NRPU and TDF schemes), NUST, Ignite, the Pakistan Science Foundation (PSF), NESCOM, Pakistan Navy, and industry.

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/department-page/funded-projects (fetched 2026-07-19)
