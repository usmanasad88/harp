# CEME Academic Programmes — NUST College of EME

The NUST College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (CEME), Rawalpindi, offers undergraduate, masters and doctoral programmes across its departments.

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/programs (fetched 2026-07-19)

## CEME bachelor's (BE) programmes

CEME offers four-year bachelor's degrees in Mechatronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Computer Engineering. The Bachelor of Mechatronics Engineering is run by the Department of Mechatronics Engineering, with curricula updated for each intake year.

## CEME MS and PhD programmes

Master's (MS) programmes: Mechatronics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Engineering Management, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Applied Mathematics.

PhD programmes: Mechatronics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Engineering Management, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Applied Mathematics.

Undergraduate and postgraduate elective course lists are published on the CEME website.
