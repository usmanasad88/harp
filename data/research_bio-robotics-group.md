# Bio Robotics Group — Mechatronics Engineering, NUST CEME

The Bio Robotics Group is a research group of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering at NUST College of EME. Members: Dr Tahir Habib Nawaz (Associate Professor — autonomous vehicles, AI, UAVs), Dr Umar Shahbaz Khan (Professor — embedded systems, UAVs), Dr Nasir Rashid (Professor — AI, UGVs), and Dr Anas bin Aqeel (Assistant Professor — additive manufacturing).

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/research/bio-robotics-group (fetched 2026-07-19)

## Bio Robotics Group — featured projects and funding

Featured projects: All Terrain Reconfigurable Tracked Vehicle (ARTV), Throwbot with Eye, Decontamination Spraying Vehicle, Fruit Orchard Spraying Vehicle, Fire Fighting UGV, TREC G3 Gun Mount UGV, IED detection and surveillance UGV, Robotic Dog, Agricultural Aerial Spraying Drones, and environmental perception for autonomous vehicles.

Funding: establishment of the Robot Design and Development Lab under NCRA (PKR 113.14 million), and "Development and Production of an Agile Quadruped Robot" (PKR 13.752 million).
