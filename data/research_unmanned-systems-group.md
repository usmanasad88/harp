# Unmanned Systems Group — Mechatronics Engineering, NUST CEME

The Unmanned Systems Group is a research group of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering at NUST College of EME, working on unmanned ground vehicles (UGVs), unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), and autonomous systems. Members: Dr Nasir Rashid (Professor — AI, UGVs), Dr Umar Shahbaz Khan (Professor — embedded systems, UAVs), and Dr Tahir Habib Nawaz (Associate Professor — autonomous vehicles, AI, UAVs).

> Source: https://ceme.nust.edu.pk/research/unmanned-systems-group (fetched 2026-07-19)

## Unmanned Systems Group — featured projects and funding

Featured projects: All Terrain Reconfigurable Tracked Vehicle (ARTV), Throwbot with Eye, Decontamination Spraying Vehicle, Fruit Orchard Spraying Vehicle, Fire Fighting UGV, TREC G3 Gun Mount UGV, IED detection and surveillance UGV, Robotic Dog, Agricultural Aerial Spraying Drones, and environmental perception for autonomous vehicles.

Funding: establishment of the Robot Design and Development Lab under NCRA (PKR 113.14 million), and "Development and Production of an Agile Quadruped Robot" (PKR 13.752 million).
