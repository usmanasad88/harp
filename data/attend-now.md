# Attend Indus RAS Expo 2026 — visitor registration

You can join Indus RAS Expo 2026 as a visitor, speaker, investor, media partner, or volunteer. Registration paths for each role are available on the expo website at https://indusrasexpo.gov.pk/attend-now. The expo is open to all visitors on 22 July 2026 from 1:00 pm to 6:00 pm and on 23 July 2026 from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm, at the Pakistan China Friendship Centre, Islamabad.
