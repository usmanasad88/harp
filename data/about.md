# About Indus RAS Expo 2026

Indus RAS Expo 2026 is Pakistan's national platform dedicated to Robotics and Autonomous Systems (RAS), bringing together government institutions, defence organizations, industry, academia, startups and innovators under one collaborative ecosystem. The Expo aims to accelerate innovation, technology adoption, skills development and strategic partnerships. It is a government-led initiative, held 22–23 July 2026 at the Pakistan China Friendship Centre, Islamabad.

> Source: https://indusrasexpo.gov.pk/about (fetched 2026-07-19)

## Why a national RAS platform for Pakistan?

Robotics and Autonomous Systems are transforming industries, defence, healthcare, agriculture and public services worldwide. To harness these opportunities, Pakistan needs a national platform that brings together government, industry, academia, startups and innovators. Indus RAS Expo provides this collaborative ecosystem to accelerate innovation and inspire future talent, positioning Pakistan as a leader in the era of intelligent and autonomous technologies.

## Indus RAS Expo — strategic intent and focus areas

Strategic intent: accelerate adoption of RAS, strengthen indigenous capability, develop talent, enable innovation, foster collaboration, and position Pakistan globally.

Focus areas: defence robotics and autonomous systems, drone technology, industrial automation, maritime autonomous systems, agritech systems, physical AI and human-machine teaming, AI decision-support systems, and startups and innovation.

## Indus RAS Expo — youth, competitions and expected outcomes

Youth and young professionals can explore emerging technologies, interact with experts, participate in workshops and discover career pathways in robotics and autonomous systems. Competitions include the RoboWars Championship and the Student Innovation Challenge, with awards and prize money.

Expected outcomes: increased awareness and adoption of RAS, stronger collaboration among stakeholders, greater opportunities for startups and innovators, enhanced youth engagement and STEM awareness, strengthened national capability in autonomous technologies, and positioning Pakistan as an emerging regional hub for RAS.

## Indus RAS Expo venue — Pakistan China Friendship Centre

The venue is the Pakistan China Friendship Centre, Garden Avenue, Shakarparian Road, Islamabad, Pakistan. Event dates: 22nd and 23rd July 2026.
