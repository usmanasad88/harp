# About Indus RAS Expo | Pakistan's Official National Platform for AI

> Source: https://indusrasexpo.gov.pk/about
> Fetched: 2026-06-30

## Overview
Indus RAS Expo 2026 is Pakistan’s National platform dedicated to Robotics and Autonomous Systems (RAS), bringing together government institutions, defence organizations, industry, academia, startups and innovators under one collaborative ecosystem. The Expo aims to accelerate innovation, technology adoption, skills development and strategic partnerships.

## Why a National RAS Platform?
Robotics and Autonomous Systems are transforming industries, defence, healthcare, agriculture and public services worldwide. To harness these opportunities, Pakistan needs a National platform that brings together government, industry, academia, startups and innovators. Indus RAS Expo provides this collaborative eco-system to accelerate innovation and inspire future talent to position Pakistan as a leader in the era of intelligent and autonomous technologies.

## INDUS RAS EXPO is a govt led Robotics & Autonomous Systems Initiative!
Indus RAS Expo serves as a National catalyst for advancing robotics and autonomous technologies in Pakistan. It provides a platform to encourage knowledge exchange, build strategic partnerships and inspire the next generation of technologists. By connecting ideas with opportunities, the initiative aims to accelerate the adoption of intelligent systems and strengthen Pakistan's position in emerging technologies.
