## Slide 11

- FUTURE RECOMMENDATIONS
- 11
- Recommendation for future research in the light of this research and results.
- The HARP platform will continue to be social interactive research platform for Human-Robot Interaction (HRI).
- Currently HARP is mobile via Tele-operation, it should be able to autonomously navigate the environment to interact with humans. This part is under research as pert of FYDP 2024-25.
- Despite significant advancements in large language models (LLMs) , few challenges remain in translating this progress into real-time conversational agents. These challenges include
- Effective scene recognition
- Seamless bilingual conversation (in both Urdu and English)
- Managing multiple simultaneous audio inputs
- Recommendation for further utilization of results/ output indicating commercialization and marketing
- Securing grants to further upgrade the system
- To expand our market reach, we plan to collaborate with commercial entities.
